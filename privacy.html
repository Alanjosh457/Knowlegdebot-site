Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Knowledge Agent Bot (“the App”) is provided by Knowledge Agent (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the App in Microsoft Teams.

1. What We Collect

We aim to minimize the data we process. Depending on how your tenant admin configures the App, we may process:

  • Messages you send to the bot (e.g., queries like hi, help, or knowledge questions) to generate responses.
  • Basic metadata such as timestamps, message IDs, and non-sensitive technical logs for reliability and troubleshooting.
  • Optional telemetry (e.g., feature usage counts) without storing personal message content, if enabled.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve the App’s features and reliability.
  • To respond to your requests, commands, and support inquiries.
  • To maintain security, prevent misuse, and comply with legal obligations.

3. What We Do Not Do

  • No selling of personal information.
  • No targeted advertising.
  • No use of your content to build advertising profiles.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Ephemeral processing: Bot messages are processed to generate a response and are retained only as needed for reliability and abuse prevention.
  • Diagnostics: We may keep aggregated, non-identifying logs for up to 30–90 days unless a longer period is required for security or legal reasons.
  • Customer content in your tenant (e.g., SharePoint/knowledge sources) remains under your organization’s control.

5. Lawful Basis

If you are in the EEA/UK, we rely on legitimate interests to provide and secure the App, or contractual necessity where we have a direct agreement with your organization. In some cases, we may rely on consent (for optional telemetry) which you can withdraw via your admin settings.

6. International Transfers

We may process data in the region where the App is hosted or where Microsoft Teams routes traffic. If data is transferred internationally, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) as applicable.

7. Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the risk, including access controls, encryption in transit, and monitoring. No system is perfectly secure; we encourage responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information, and to object or port data. To exercise rights, contact your Microsoft 365/Teams administrator or email us at . We will cooperate with your organization’s policies.

9. Children

The App is intended for enterprise and organizational use only and is not directed to children under 13 (or the age defined by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Third Parties

The App runs within Microsoft Teams and may integrate with Microsoft services and your organization’s systems (e.g., SharePoint, Azure). Those services are governed by their own terms and privacy notices. Your organization controls tenant configurations and identity access.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent changes. Material changes will be communicated via the App or your admin.
